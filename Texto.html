Model of the week:
SORA CHOI

Who is Sora Choi?

Sora Choi (최소라), born on September 5th 1992, is a South Korean fashion model. She started her modeling career when she was a junior in high school.

Choi studied modeling at Dongduk Women's University, one of the top schools for female fashion in South Korea.he became a prominent young model in Korea's fashion scene as she won the third cycle of the television show Korea's Next Top Model in 2012.

Early on in her career, Choi only worked within Korea's fashion industry until she received a callback request from Louis Vuitton. She became the first South Korean model to ever be chosen as Louis Vuitton's city exclusive for Paris Fashion Week, handpicked by Nicolas Ghesquiere himself.

In 2017, she was listed on models.com's "Top 50 models" list. Choi has since walked for brands such as Prada, Gucci, Miu Miu, Chanel, Saint Laurent, Hermès, Alexander McQueen, Givenchy, Lanvin, Dior, Celine, Moschino, Valentino, Proenza Schouler, Loewe, Michael Kors, Paco Rabanne, Marc Jacobs, Burberry, Balenciaga and many others.

In December 2020, Choi won models.com's "Readers' Choice Model of the Year" award. Since 2021, she has been ranked as an "Industry Icon". In October 2021, she received the Minister of Culture, Sports and Tourism Commendation at the Korean Popular Culture and Arts Awards. In October 2022, she joined The Business of Fashion's "The BoF 500", a list of people that are considered most influential in shaping the global fashion industry.

5 facts about Sora Choi:

1. She was discovered after she tagged along with her friend to a meeting at YGKPlus, a South Korean modeling agency. Instead of her friend, she was the one who ended up getting signed.

2. Sora grew up with a passion for for art and loved to paint. She even thought of becoming a professional artist.

3. Before becoming a fashion model, she had never thought of working in the fashion industry.

4. Besides being a workaholic, she loves reading manga and videogames. She likes playing League of Legends, Warcraft and Blade & Soul.

5. Sora follows an strict diet, which impacts a lot on her health: red spots apear all over body, her skin gets dry and she has to deal with intestinal inflammation induced by stress. Sora also often faints due to the lack of energy.
